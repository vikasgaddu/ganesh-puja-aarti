श्री गणेश आरती — सुखकर्ता दुःखहर्ता
Shri Ganesh Aarti — Sukhakarta Dukhharta
देवनागरी IAST English
सुखकर्ता दुःखहर्ता वार्ता विघ्नाची । Sukhakartā duḥkhahartā vārtā vighnācī Bringer of joy, remover of sorrow, ender of obstacles.
नुरवी पुरवी प्रेम कृपा जयाची ॥ Nuravī puravī prema kṛpā jāyacī He nourishes and fulfills; His love and grace bring victory.
सर्वांगी सुंदर उटी शेंदुराची । Sarvāṅgī sundara ūṭī śendurācī Beautiful in every limb, anointed with vermilion.
कंठी झळके माळा मुक्ताफळांची ॥ Kaṇṭhī jhalake mālā muktāphalañcī Around His neck gleams a garland of pearls.
Chorus (धृुव)
जय देव जय देव जय मंगलमूर्ति ।
Jaya deva jaya deva jaya maṅgalamūrti
Glory to You, O auspicious form!

दर्शनमात्रे मनकामना पूर्ति ॥
Darśanamātre manakāmanā pūrti
By Your mere darshan, desires are fulfilled.
रत्नखचित फरा तुज गौरीकुमारा । Ratnakhacit phāra tuja Gaurikumara O son of Gauri, a jewel-studded frontal plate adorns You.
चंदनाची उटी कुंकुमकेशरा ॥ Candānācī ūṭī kuṅkuma-keśarā Forehead anointed with sandal and saffron.
हीरजडीत मुकुट शोभतो बरा । Hīrājāḍīta mukuṭa śobhato barā A diamond-studded crown shines splendidly.
रूंजुनती नुपुरे चरणी घागरिया ॥ Rūñjunatī nupure caraṇī ghāgariyā Anklets jingle at His feet.
लंबोदर पीतांबर फणिवरवंदना । Lambodara pitāmbara phaṇivaravandanā Broad-bellied, clad in yellow; praised by the serpent king.
सरळ सोंड वक्रतुंड त्रिनयना ॥ Saraḷa soṇḍa vakratuṇḍa trinayanā Straight trunk, curved-snouted, three-eyed Lord.
दास रामाचा वाट पाहे सदना । Dāsa Rāmācā vāṭa pāhē sadanā Your servant Rāma's (devotee) waits at Your abode.
संकटी पावावे निर्वणी रक्षवे सुरवरवंदना ॥ Saṅkaṭī pāvavē nirvāṇī rakṣavē suravara-vandanā In calamity, rescue us; grant liberation; the gods bow to You.
श्री गणेश आरती — शेंदूर लाल चढ़ायो
Shri Ganesh Aarti — Shendur Lāl Chaḍhāyo
शेंदुर लाल चढ़ायो अच्छा गजमुखको । Śendura lāla chaḍhāyo acchā gajamukhako Fine vermilion is offered to the auspicious, elephant-faced Lord.
दोंडिल लाल बिराजे सुत गौरीहरको ॥ Doṇḍila lāla birāje sut Gauriharako Ruby-hued son of Gaurī's Lord sits enthroned.
हाथ लिये गुड़ लड्डू साईं सुरवरको । Hātha liye guḍ-laḍḍū sāīṃ suravara-ko He holds jaggery laddus, delight of the gods.
महिमा कहे न जाय लागत हूं पादको ॥१॥ Mahimā kahe na jāya, lāgata hūṃ pāda-ko ||1|| His glory is beyond words; I bow at His feet.
Chorus (धृुव)
जय जय श्री गणराज विद्या सुखदाता ।
Jaya jaya Śrī Gaṇarāja vidyā-sukhadātā
Glory to Shri Ganaraj, giver of wisdom and joy.

धन्य तुम्हारा दर्शन मेरा मन रमता ॥
Dhanya tumhārā darśana, merā mana ramatā
Blessed is Your darshan; my heart rejoices.
अष्ट सिद्धि दासी संकटको बैरी । Aṣṭa siddhi dāsī, saṅkaṭa-ko bairī The eight siddhis serve You; You are the foe of troubles.
विघ्नविनाशन मंगलमूर्ति अधिकारी । Vighna-vināśana, maṅgala-mūrti adhikārī Remover of obstacles; embodiment of auspiciousness.
कोटि सूरज प्रकाश ऐसी छवि तेरी । Koṭi sūraj prakāś, aisī chhavi terī Your radiance equals millions of suns; Your splendor is flawless.
गंडस्थल मदमस्तक झूले शशिबिहारी ॥२॥ Gaṇḍa-sthala mada-mastaka jhūle Śaśi-bihārī ||2|| With shining cheeks and moon-adorned head, You sway gracefully.
भाव भक्ति से कोई शरणागत आवे। Bhāva-bhakti se koī śaraṇāgata āve Whoever comes with devotion into Your refuge…
संतत संपत्ति सबहि भरपूर पावे। Santata sampat sabahi bharpūr pāve …receives constant wealth and abundance.
ऐसे तुम महाराज मोको अति भावे। Aise tum Mahārāj moko ati bhāve O Great King, thus You are most dear to me.
गोसावीनंदन निशिदिन गुण गावे॥३॥ Gosāvinandana, niśi-dina guṇa gāve ||3|| O son of the ascetic, may Your virtues be sung day and night.
श्री दुर्गा आरती — दुर्गे दुर्घट भारी
Devi Aarti — Durge Durghat Bhari
दुर्गे दुर्घट भारी तुजविण संसारी । Durgē durghaṭa bhārī tujavina saṃsārī O Durgā, without You the world is terribly difficult.
अनाथनाथे अंबे करुणा विस्तारी ॥ Anāthanāthē ambē karuṇā vistārī Mother of the helpless, Ambā—extend Your compassion.
वारी वारीं जन्ममरणाते वारी । Vārī vārīṃ janma-maraṇātē vārī Again and again deliver us from birth and death.
हारी पडलो आता संकट नीवारी ॥१॥ Hārī paḍalō ātā saṅkaṭa nivārī ||1|| I have fallen defeated—now remove my troubles.
Chorus (धृुव)
जय देवी जय देवी जय महिषासुरमथनी ।
Jaya dēvī jaya dēvī jaya mahiṣāsura-mathanī
Victory to the Goddess, slayer of Mahīṣāsura!

सुरवरईश्वरवरदे तारक संजीवनी ॥
Suravara-īśvara-varadē tāraka saṃjīvanī
Giver of boons to gods and to Īśvara—saviour, life-giver.
त्रिभुवनी भुवनी पाहतां तुज ऐसे नाही । Tribhuvanī bhuvanī pāhatāṃ tuja aisé nāhī In the three worlds none like You is seen.
चारी श्रमले परंतु न बोलावे काहीं॥ Cārī śramalē parantu na bōlāvē kāhīṃ The four (Vedas/sages) grew weary yet could not speak Your greatness.
साही विवाद करितां पडिले प्रवाही । Sāhī vivāda karitāṃ paḍilē pravāhī Those who argued were swept away like a current.
ते तूं भक्तलागी पावसि लवलाही ॥२॥ Tē tūṃ bhaktā-lāgī pāvasi lavalāhī ||2|| Yet for Your devotees You arrive swiftly.
प्रसन्न वदने प्रसन्न होसी निजदासां । Prasanna vadanē prasanna hōsī nija-dāsāṃ With a gracious face, be gracious to Your servants.
क्लेशापासूनी सोडी तोडी भवपाशा ॥ Klēśāpasūni sōḍī tōḍī bhava-pāśā Free us from afflictions; cut the noose of saṃsāra.
अंबे तुजवांचून कोण पुरविल आशा । Ambē tujavāñcūn kōṇa puravil āśā Ambā, who besides You can fulfill our hopes?
नरहरि तल्लीन झाला पदपंकजलेशा ॥३॥ Narahari tallina jhālā pada-paṅkaja-lēśā ||3|| Narahari is absorbed in the dust of Your lotus feet.
श्री शंकर आरती — लावथवती विकरळा
Shiva Aarti — Lavthavati Vikrala
लावथवती विकरळा ब्रह्मांडी माळा । Lavthavatī vikrāḷā brahmāṇḍī māḷā Formidable One, garlanded with the universe.
वीषें कंठ काळा त्रिनेत्री ज्वाळा ॥ Vīṣeṃ kaṇṭha kāḷā trinetṛīṃ jvāḷā With poison-darkened throat and blazing third eye.
लावण्यसुंदर मस्तकीं बाळा । Lāvaṇya-sundara mastakīṃ bāḷā Upon the beautiful head sits the youthful Lord.
तेथुनियां जल निर्मळ वाहे झुळझुळां॥१॥ Tēthuniyāṃ jala nirmaḷa vāhē jhuḷjhuḷāṃ ||1|| From there pure waters flow in ripples.
Chorus (धृुव)
जय देव जय देव जय श्रीशंकरा ।
Jaya dēva jaya dēva jaya śrīśaṅkarā
Victory to Shri Shankara!

आरती ओवाळूं तुज कर्पूरगौरा ॥
Āratī ovāḷūṃ tuja karpūragaurā
We wave the camphor lamp to You, O fair as camphor.
कर्पूरगौरा भोळा नयनीं विशाळा । Karpūragaurā bhoḷā naynīṃ viśāḷā Pure as camphor, simple, with large eyes.
अर्धांगीं पार्वती सुमनांच्या माळा ॥ Ardhāṅgīṃ Pārvatī sumanāñcyā māḷā Half Your form is Parvati, with garlands of blossoms.
विभूतीचे उधळण शितिकंठ नीळा । Vibhūtīcēṃ udhaḷaṇa śitikaṇṭha nīḷā Sacred ash scattered over Your cool blue throat.
ऐसा शंकर शोभे उमावेलहळा ॥२॥ Aisā Śaṅkara śōbhē Umā-velhāḷā ||2|| Such Shiva shines, beloved of Uma.
देवीं दैत्यीं सागरमंथन पै केले। Dēvīṃ daityīṃ sāgara-manthana pai kēlēṃ During the churning of the ocean by gods and demons…
त्यामाजीं जे अवचित हळाहळ उठिले॥ Tyāmājīṃ jēṃ avacita haḷāhaḷa uṭhilēṃ When the sudden deadly poison arose…
ते तूं असुरपणे प्राशन केले। Tēṃ tūṃ asurapaṇēṃ prāśana kēlēṃ You compassionately drank it…
नीळकंठ नाम प्रसिद्ध झाले॥३॥ Nīḷakaṇṭha nāma prasiddha jhālēṃ ||3|| …and became famed as Nīlakaṇṭha.
व्याघ्रांबर फणिवरधर सुंदर मदनारी । Vyāghrāṃbara phaṇivaradhara sundara madanārī Clad in tiger-skin, serpent as ornament; consort most beautiful.
पंचानन मनमोहन मुनिजनसुखकारी ॥ Pañcānana manamohana munijana-sukhakārī Five-faced, mind-enchanting, giver of sages' joy.
शतकोटींचे बीज वाचे उच्चरी । Śatakoṭīcēṃ bīja vāce ucchārī Uttering seed-mantras of countless crores.
रघुकुळटिळक रामदासा अंतरीं॥४॥ Raghukuḷa-ṭiḷaka Rāmadāsā antarīṃ ||4|| Ramdas, ornament of the Raghu line, abides within.
श्री विठ्ठल आरती — युगे अठ्ठावीस
Panduranga Aarti — Yuge Aṭṭhāvis
युगे अठ्ठावीस विटेवरी उभा । Yugē aṭṭhāvis viṭēvarī ubhā Standing through twenty-eight yugas upon the brick.
वामांगी रखुमाई दिसे दिव्य शोभा । Vāmāṅgī Rakhumāī disē divya śobhā On His left stands Rakhumāī in divine beauty.
पुंडलिकाचे भेटी परब्रह्म आले गा । Puṇḍalikāce bhēṭī parabrahma ālē gā Parabrahma came to meet Puṇḍalik.
चरणीं वाहे भीमा उद्धरी जगा ।। १ ।। Caraṇīṃ vāhē Bhīmā uddharī jagā ||1|| At His feet flows the Bhīmā, uplifting the world.
Chorus (धृुव)
जय देव जय देव जय पांडुरंगा ।
Jaya dēva jaya dēva jaya Pāṇḍuraṅgā
Glory to Lord Pāṇḍuraṅga!

रखुमाईवल्लभा राईच्या वल्लभा पावे जीवलगा ॥
Rakhumāī-vallabhā, Rāīcyā vallabhā pāvē jivalagā
Beloved of Rakhumāī and Rāī—accept our devoted hearts.
तुळसीमाळा गळां कर ठेवुनि कटीं। Tuḷasī-māḷā gaḷāṃ kara ṭhēvuṇi kaṭīṃ Tulsi garland at the neck; hands on the waist.
कांसे पीतांबर कस्तुरी ललाटी । Kānse pītāṃbara kasturī lallāṭī Golden-yellow robes; musk on the brow.
देव सुरवर नित्य येती भेटी । Dēva suravara nitya yētī bhēṭī Gods and celestials come daily for darshan.
गरुड हनुमन्त पुढे उभे राहती ।। २ ।। Garuḍa Hanumanta puḍhē ubhē rāhatī ||2|| Garuda and Hanuman stand before Him.
धन्य वेणुनाद अनुक्षेत्रपाळा । Dhanya vēṇunāda anu-kṣētrapāḷā Blessed is the flute-melody; guardian of sacred lands.
सुवर्णाची कमळे वनमाळा गळां । Suvarṇācī kamaḷe vanamāḷā gaḷāṃ Golden lotuses; forest garlands at the neck.
राही रखुमाबाई राणीया सकळा । Rāhī Rakhumābāī rāṇīyā sakaḷā With Rāhī, Rakhumābāī, and all queens.
ओवाळिती राजा विठोबा सावळा ।। ३ ।। Ovāliti rājā Viṭhobā sāvaḷā ||3|| They wave lamps to the dark-hued king Viṭhobā.
ओवाळूं आरत्या कुरवंड्या येती । Ovālūṃ āratyā kurvaṇḍyā yētī They perform āratī; the little lamps arrive.
चंद्रभागेमाजी सोडुनियां देती । Caṃdrabhāgēmājī sōḍuniyāṃ dētī They set them afloat on the Chandrabhāga.
डिंडया पताका वैष्णव नाचती । Diṇḍyā patākā vaiṣṇava nācatī Vaishnavs dance with processional flags.
पंढरीचा महिमा वर्णावा किती ।। ४ ।। Paṇḍharīcā mahimā varṇāvā kitī ||4|| How can Pandharpur's glory be described?
आषाढी कार्तिकी भक्तजन येती । Āṣāḍhī kārtikī bhakta-jana yētī In Āṣāḍhī and Kārtikī, devotees gather.
चंद्रभागेमध्ये स्नान जे करिती । Caṃdrabhāgē-madhyē snāna jē karitī They bathe in the Chandrabhāga.
दर्शनहेळामात्रे त्यां होय मुक्ति। Darśana-hēḷā-mātrē tyāṃ hōy mukti By the mere sight (of Him) they gain liberation.
केशवासी नामदेव भावे ओवाळिती ।। ५ ।। Keśavāsī Nāmdev bhāvē ovāḷitī ||5|| Keśavāsī Nāmdev offers this āratī with devotion.
मन्त्र पुष्पांजली
Mantra Pushpanjali (Vedic Verses)
ॐ यज्ञेन यज्ञमयजन्त देवास्तानि धर्माणि प्रथमान्यासन्। om yajñena yajñam ayajanta devās tāni dharmāṇi prathamāny āsan By sacrifice the gods performed sacrifice; those were the earliest ordinances.
ते ह नाकं महिमानः सचन्त यत्र पूर्वे साध्यासन्ति देवाः॥१॥ te ha nākaṃ mahimānaḥ sacanta yatra pūrve sādhyāḥ santi devāḥ ||1|| They attained heaven's height where the ancient Sādhyas dwell.
ॐ राजाधिराजाय प्रसह्यसाहिने नमो वयं वैश्रवणाय कुर्महे। om rājādhirājāya prasahya-sāhine namo vayaṃ vaiśravaṇāya kurmahe We bow to Rājādhirāja, the mighty Vaiśravaṇa (Kubera).
स मे कामान्कामकामाय महयम् कामेश्वरो वैश्रवणो ददातु। sa me kāmān kāma-kāmāya mahyam kāmeśvaro vaiśravaṇo dadātu May the Lord of desires, Vaiśravaṇa, grant me my worthy desires.
कुबेराय वैश्रवणाय महाराजाय नमः॥२॥ kuberāya vaiśravaṇāya mahārājāya namaḥ ||2|| Salutations to Kubera, Great King Vaiśravaṇa.
ॐ स्वस्ति । सामराज्यं भौज्यं स्वराज्यं वैराज्यं पारमेष्ठ्यं राज्यं महाराज्यमाधिपत्यमयं । om svasti | sāmrājyaṃ bhaujyaṃ svārājyaṃ vairājyaṃ pārameṣṭhyaṃ rājyaṃ mahārājyam ādhipatyam ayaṃ Auspiciousness: may there be sovereignty, enjoyment, independence, supreme status, kingship, great kingship and lordship.
समन्तपर्यायी स्यात्सार्वभौमः सार्वायुष आन्तादापरार्धात्पृथिव्यै समुद्रपर्यन्तायाम् एकराळिति ॥३॥ samanta-paryāyī syāt sārvabhaumaḥ sārvāyuṣ āntād āparārdhāt pṛthivyai samudra-paryantāyām eka-rāḷ iti ||3|| May it extend on all sides, be universal and long-lived—up to the ocean-girt earth.
तदप्येषः श्लोकोऽभिगीतो । मरुतः परिवेष्टारो मरुत्स्वावसन्गृहे। tad apyeṣaḥ śloko 'bhigīto | marutaḥ pariveṣṭāro marutsy āvasan gṛhe Moreover this verse is sung: 'The Maruts attended, dwelling in Marut's house'—
आविक्षितस्य कामप्रेर्विश्वेदेवाः सभासद इति ॥४॥ āvikṣitasya kāmaprer viśvedevāḥ sabhāsada iti ||4|| '…for the desire-fulfilling king; all the gods were seated in assembly.'
